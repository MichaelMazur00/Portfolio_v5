Platform experience

Leading the redesign of a payments platform — driving a 93% lift in customer engagement and a 21% drop in Connected account-related support contacts.

The Stripe platform dashboard is the daily home for enterprise customers managing payments at scale — but it was failing them. Accounts showed "working" while unable to process. Restricted accounts gave no reason or path to fix. Execs, growth, and compliance leadership had no tooling at all. Remediators had a UI but called it busted. A banking sponsor change spotlit these gaps and fast-tracked the work.

As solo designer, I led the year-long redesign — setting vision and roadmap, presenting through to executive review. The work rebuilt the information architecture around real user workflows, introduced first-time tooling for execs, growth, and compliance leadership, and overhauled the busted remediator UI. Result: 93% engagement lift (3+ logins per week), 21% drop in account support contacts.
